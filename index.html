Server Commands

  • /spawn - Teleports you to the server spawn point.
  • /home - Teleports you to your set home.
  • /sethome - Sets your current location as your home.
  • /warp [warpname] - Warps you to a set location on the map.
  • /tpa [playername] - Sends a teleport request to another player.
  • /tpaccept & /tpadeny - Accepts / denys a pending teleport request.
  • /enderchest - Lets you access your ender chest from anywhere.
  • /ghastpark & /ghastunpark - Parks and unparks the nearest happy ghast.
  • /rtp - Teleports you to a random location in the wilderness (random teleport).
  • /helpbc - More ArkMC Commands.
  • /server [ArkMC, hub, Skyblock, Prisons] - Teleport to the other gamemodes

These are the ArkMC exclusive commands, added via a plugin I coded. More commands and detailed usage can be found in-game or by asking in the Discord server.

How to Stop Your Base from Getting Griefed

Protecting your creations is crucial on any survival server. While Ark Network has rules against griefing, taking proactive steps ensures your base remains safe and secure. Follow these simple steps to claim your land and prevent unwanted destruction!

  1. Acquire a Golden Shovel: Craft or find a golden shovel in the game. This is your primary tool for land claiming.
  2. Right-Click a Block: With the golden shovel in hand, right-click on one corner of the area you wish to protect. This marks the first point of your claim.
  3. Right-Click Another Block: Move to the diagonally opposite corner of the area you want to claim and right-click with the golden shovel again. This marks the second point, completing your claim.
  4. Verify Your Claim: A message will appear confirming your claim and showing you the number of blocks claimed. You can also see the claim boundaries.
  5. Expand or Resize (Optional): To expand your claim, simply right-click outside your current claim with the golden shovel. To resize, right-click inside.
  6. Trust Players (Optional): To allow friends to build in your claimed area, use the command /trust [playername].
  7. Untrust Players (Optional): To remove a player's building permissions, use the command /untrust [playername].

If you need more claimed blocks to claim a bigger area, ask me on the Discord Server.
